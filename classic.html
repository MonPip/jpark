About Me

I started in biochemistry and physics, left to spend years in social work and education, then came back through electrical engineering and landed in product management. The winding path wasn't the plan, but it's the reason I'm good at what I do.

I think in systems. I can sit with engineers and debate architecture, then turn around and make the case to stakeholders in language that actually lands. When I want to validate an idea, I don't write a spec and wait. I build it.
